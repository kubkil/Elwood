Handmade wooden rings from Poland.
You can find us on Etsy

In 2016 we launched our first project - Spirit Workshop. Now many experiences richer we are ready for a new one - Elwood.

Elwood was created to fulfill a desire for crafting, making something beautiful and useful by combining natural materials with unique designs.

We make rings from all kinds of local and exotic wood (tineo, mahogany, rosewood, cherry, maple, walnut - to name a few) using traditional woodcrafting methods (like wood bending) and all items are handmade in Poland.

Long story short - first the wood is hardened with a special curing agent, next it is shaped and sanded by hand for the right look and size. At the end, we varnish each ring for additional resistance and waterproofness.

By approaching each ring individually and with unusual care we can offer you rings that you won't find in any shopping center or chain store.
