Handmade wooden rings from Poland. You can find us on Etsy

In 2016 we launched our first project - Spirit Workshop. Now many experiences richer we are ready for a new one - Elwood.

Elwood was created to fulfill a desire for crafting, making something beautiful and useful by combining natural materials with unique designs.

We make jewellery from all kind of local and exotic wood using traditional woodcrafting methods and all items are handmade in Poland.
